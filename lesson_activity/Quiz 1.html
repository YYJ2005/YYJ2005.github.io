Simple Spange Cake
  1. Ingredients
    • 3 eggs
    • 100g castor sugar
    • 85g self-raising flour
Notes on Ingredients
Custor Sugar
Finely granulated withe sugar
Self-raising Flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavning agents(usually salt and baking powder.)
    Title
  1. Preheat the oven to 190oC
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven until the top cake springs back when lightly presses.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
