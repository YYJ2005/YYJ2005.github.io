My Favourite Game

Mobile Legends

My favourite game is Moblie Legends. It is because the game has only one goal: real and destory the enemy's base to win. This is a 5 VS 5 match, and victoryis imposibble without teamwork. Teamwork strategy, the right hero selection and perfect timing are the keys to winning the game. As I said before, catching up on a losing game is only possible with the help of solid teamwork.

After being on hiatus from playing the game, I saw myself reinstalling it. I don't know the exact reason why but I could say that it's really fun challenging yourself with diffrent roles and heroes you want to and master. It's not an addiction, it's not an obsession but I see this as learning and developing mental strategies and social ability with different kinds of people. I think this makes the game more fun and popular, the idea of establishing virtual friendship and connection.
